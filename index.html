Welcome to Be Well

Sometimes it's not easy to ask for help but we believe we can be the first step on the path of self help. Something as simple as gaining a personal routine can feel like a major tifumph in a world that can make you feel defeated. You can use Be Well as a tool to get your life back in order and find a resourse that fits your individual need. It's not always easy to ask for help but looking for answers is a huge first step on your path to self improvement. Learn more about us be for you dive into our content. We're here for you.

About Be well Reminders

We're available when you need us most.

With your phone number you are able to sign up for Be Well Reminders. They allow the user to keep in mind the important things when they need them most. From normal day-to-day hygine reminders to personalized lifesyle choices. You can be reminded of random mantras to remind you of the positive things about yourslef you may forget from time to time. Life can get tough but Be Well Reminders can help keep you looking on the brighter side of life. Anything worth doing is worth doing poorly. Even a small step in the right direction is still a step towards sucess.

Discussion Board

At Be Well we understand that sometimes the best way to start your journey is to connect with people who know exactly what you're dealing with. With our Discussion Board you can connect to individuals through topic channels. If you do not find a topic on your issue you can always anonymously start your own. This allows people to freely and safely comunicate about sensitive issues they thought no one could understant. By realizing others are going through similar issues if can help you realize you are not alone and hopeully ease you into having a harder conversation with those closest to you in your lives.

Anonymously connect with those who understand your concerns best.

Ask for Help!

Never be afraid to get the help you need!

While we pride ourself in being a useful first step on your journey we highly encourage the use of professional help. There are a variety of resources available in most areas for people who need the help but may not be able to easily acess it. If talking to a professional is still not an option at least getting in contact with a trusted family member, friend, or advisor. You are never alone and there are people who care. all you have to do is be willing to ask.
