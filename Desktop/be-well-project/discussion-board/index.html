Family Problems..
I’ve been feeling extremely depressed lately and have tried to reach out to loved ones, but they just don’t seem to understand and think I’m just having a bad day or exaggerating my feelings. How do I get them to understand what I’m going through when they don’t believe that depression is a real issue?
It hurts, but not everyone is going to understand what you’re going through. Find a couple people who do understand and who you trust such as a friend, therapist, etc. and rely on them. When the time seems right, look at a resource such as https://www.thephoenixrc.com/how-to-explain-depression-to-a-loved-one-who-doesnt-understand/ and attempt to have a calm, informative conversation.
